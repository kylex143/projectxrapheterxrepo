Body Overview of Number Systems Binary System: Utilizes two digits (0, 1) and is the foundation of computer operations. Octal System: Comprises eight digits (0-7) and is often used in computing for compact representation. Decimal System: The most familiar system, using ten digits (0-9), prevalent in everyday life. Hexadecimal System: Employs sixteen symbols (0-9, A-F) and is commonly used in programming for memory addressing(Rawat et al., 2012) (Latif et al., 2011).
